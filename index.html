Is all about understanding what kids read! They learn to sound out words, recognize common words by sight, and answer simple questions about stories. Kids learn to count from 1 to 100, recognize numbers, and count objects. They also use counting in everyday activities and start to understand how numbers compare and relate to each other. It's a fun way to build a strong foundation for math!
